This report presents the structural assessment of the steel frame for the Residential Tower A project. The analysis covers all primary load-bearing elements including beams, columns, and connections. The following load combinations have been considered in the analysis. Permanent loads include self-weight of the structure and floor finishing. Variable loads include imposed loads according to NEN-EN 1991-1-1 category A (residential).

Re: Open-source tooling for building permit assessment
Dear Sir/Madam,

Thank you for your inquiry regarding the OpenAEC Foundation's tools for automated building permit assessment. We are pleased to share our approach to open-source compliance checking against the Dutch Building Decree (Bouwbesluit 2012).

Our rule engine processes IFC building models and validates them against a configurable set of regulatory requirements. The system is designed to be transparent: every check is traceable to a specific article in the regulation, and all source code is publicly auditable. This ensures that the assessment process is reproducible and verifiable by any party.

We would welcome the opportunity to present a demonstration of the toolset and discuss potential collaboration. The foundation operates on a non-profit basis, and our mission is to make professional AEC tools accessible to everyone — including government bodies.

We look forward to your response.

## Print Recommendations

- **Body text on white paper:** Use Deep Forge (K:76) or 100% Black
- **Amber accents:** May need bump in Yellow channel on uncoated stock
- **Dark backgrounds (Night Build):** Consider rich black: C:40 M:30 Y:30 K:100
- **Minimum font size for amber text on white:** 10pt (contrast concern)
- **Minimum line weight for amber lines:** 0.5pt
